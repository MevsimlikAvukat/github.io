Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Sort Heist 3D

  • Last updated: 2026-04-16
  • Sort Heist 3D respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the game.

1. Data We Collectr

  • Sort Heist 3D does not require account creation or login.
  • The game itself does not ask you to manually submit personal information such as your name, address, or phone number.
  • However, the app uses third-party services for ads, analytics, crash diagnostics, fraud prevention, and app delivery. These services may process certain technical information, including:
  • - Device identifiers
  • - Advertising identifiers
  • - IP address
  • - App version
  • - Device model
  • - Operating system version
  • - Language and region
  • - Basic gameplay or app interaction events
  • - Crash and diagnostics information

2. Why This Data May Be Processed

This information may be used for:

  • - Serving rewarded and interstitial ads
  • - Preventing fraud and abuse
  • - Measuring ad performance
  • - Improving stability and app performance
  • - Diagnosing crashes and technical issues

    • 3. Third-Party Services

    Sort Heist 3D may use third-party SDKs and services, including ad mediation and advertising partners.

    These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

    Examples include:

  • - Unity / LevelPlay / ironSource services
  • - Advertising and mediation partners enabled through the app's monetization setup

    • 4. Children's Privacy

    Sort Heist 3D is not designed specifically for children under 13.

    The app is not marketed as a children's app.

    5. Your Choices

    Depending on your device and region, you may be able to:

  • - Limit ad tracking
  • - Reset your advertising ID
  • - Control permissions through your device settings

    • 6. Data Security

    We take reasonable steps to work with services that protect data in transit and process data securely.

    However, no transmission method or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100 percent secure.

    7. Changes to This Policy

    This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

    If changes are made, the updated version will be posted at the public policy URL used for the app.

    8. Contact

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

  • info@arcurastudio.com
